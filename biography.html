Biography

  • I was born in 1991, Anhui, China. I grew up in Hefei.

  • I received the B. Eng. degree in Electrical Engineering,12 Zhejiang University, 2012.

  • I received the M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering: Systems, University of Michigan, 2013.

  • I received the PhD degree in Electrical Engineering: Systems at the University of Michigan, 2017, supervised by Prof. Stéphane Lafortune.

  • I joined the Department of Automation, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017, where I am currently an Associate Professor.

  • In the summer of 2014, I was an intern in Supervisory Control and Systems Integration Lab, at GE Global Research, Niskayuna, NY, US.

  • My Erdös Number is 4 (through Stéphane Lafortune -> Peter E. Caines -> László Gerencsér -> Paul Erdös).
